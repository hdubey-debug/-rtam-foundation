# RTAM Foundation — Usage Rules (skeleton)

> Structural skeleton committed in **US-1**. Full content authored in **US-9**.

## Quick rules (placeholder — fully expanded in US-9)

- Always use SVG for digital, never raster, for the logo.
- The bindu under R is part of the wordmark — never move, recolor, or remove it independently.
- Logo must remain legible in pure black at favicon size.
- Do not place the wordmark on busy or low-contrast backgrounds.
- Do not stretch, compress, rotate, or apply shadows / glows / gradients to the mark.
- Do not combine the wordmark with separate religious iconography (lotus, flame, Om, trishul, etc.).
