Primary wordmark — sacred & public variants

Five SVG variants of the primary wordmark. The under-dot beneath the R is drawn as a separate <circle> — it is the brand signature, not a Unicode combining mark.

1 · Sacred (charcoal letters, gold bindu) — canonical primary mark on ivory
2 · All-gold — premium / ceremonial
3 · Pure-black — single-color print, fax, stamp, embossing
4 · Ivory on charcoal — dark backgrounds
4b · Ivory on deep indigo (sacred night)
5 · Public ASCII (no bindu) — URLs, email signatures, legal documents
