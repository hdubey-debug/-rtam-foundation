26 May 2026

To the trustees,

The works of the foundation this quarter have proceeded along three lines: the preservation of the temple precincts, the publication of two short commentaries, and the convening of the Vaiśākha study circle for thirty participants.

The accompanying report records each in detail. We ask that the trustees approve the proposed allocation for the coming quarter, with the bulk directed to the temple's annual renovation cycle.

The work continues steadily, in keeping with the order it intends to serve.

— The Secretariat

For and on behalf of the RTAM Foundation
